How CertiChain Works

CertiChain revolutionizes certificate issuance and verification through blockchain technology, ensuring trust, security, and immutability for institutions and users.

Institution Registration & Verification

Institutions register on CertiChain with official documents and accreditation details. The system automatically verifies the institution through authoritative external databases to ensure legitimacy.

Learn More

Certificate Issuance & Blockchain Registration

After verification, institutions issue certificates that are digitally signed and registered on the blockchain, ensuring that they are tamper-proof and easily verifiable.

Learn More

Certificate Verification & Validation

Users or organizations can easily verify the authenticity of certificates on CertiChain by accessing the blockchain, ensuring that the certificates are genuine and unaltered.
